**What the problem is:** AI search engines synthesize answers from their training data and real-time web sources. If your firm isn't in those data streams, you don't exist to anyone using AI to research the market.

**What clients get:**
- Reverse-engineering of LLM outputs to identify which sources and entities the models prioritize for CRE queries in your market
- Targeted content injection into the publications, data sources, and retrieval pipelines that feed these models
- Monthly citation monitoring across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, Copilot — with correction if a model hallucinates or drops your ranking

**Timeline:** Early brand mentions within 60–90 days. Deeper training data integration within 3–6 months.

**Key distinction:** AEO = Answer Engine Optimization (Google featured snippets). GEO = Generative Engine Optimization (LLM outputs). We do both.

## Brand Voice & Tone

**Direct.** No filler. No corporate speak. Say the specific thing.

**Data-driven.** Ground every claim in real numbers — cap rates, keyword volumes, response windows, view counts. CRE operators don't respect vague claims.

**Peer-level.** Promperty speaks as a fellow operator who understands the mechanics of commercial real estate — not as a vendor pitching software. Reference deal cycles, IRR thresholds, absorption data, and submarket dynamics naturally.

**Action-oriented.** Focus on outcomes (deal flow, investor inquiries, booked meetings) not process (we do X, Y, Z steps).

**Honest about the problem.** Name the competitor (LoopNet). Name the platform (ChatGPT). Name the statistic (60% of leads lost to slow response). Credibility comes from specificity.
